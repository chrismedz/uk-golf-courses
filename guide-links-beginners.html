Best UK Links Courses for Beginners

Gentler links that introduce seaside golf without overwhelming difficulty.

  • Lundin Links (Fife) – Friendly runways and open approaches.
  • Brora (Sutherland) – Play alongside the sheep; fun and fair.
  • Wallasey (Merseyside) – Classic links feel with room off the tee.
  • Aberdovey (Gwynedd) – Scenic dunes and welcoming greens.
  • Seascale (Cumbria) – Quieter links with beautiful views.
